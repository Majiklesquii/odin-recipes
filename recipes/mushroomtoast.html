Mushrooms on Toast

Description

Below lies a delicious simplified recipe for mushrooms on toast, a hearty, (even decadent), breakfast or lunch option.

Ingredients

  • 100g sliced chestnut or portobello mushrooms
  • 2 cloves chopped garlic
  • One slice toast
  • 40g butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. In a frying pan, heat the butter on a medium heat.
  2. Add chopped garlic, and cook until fragrant
  3. Add mushrooms, cook until browned and soft. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Empty contents of pan onto toast
